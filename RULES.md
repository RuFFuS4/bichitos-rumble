# Game Rules — Bichitos Rumble

## Objective
Be the last critter standing in the arena.

## Match Format
- 4 critters per match
- 90-second time limit
- Circular arena that collapses every 15 seconds

## Controls
- **WASD / Arrow Keys**: Move your critter
- **Space**: Headbutt attack (short cooldown)
- **J**: Ability 1 (unique per critter)
- **K**: Ability 2 (unique per critter)
- **R**: Restart (after match ends)

## Mechanics
- **Headbutt**: Knocks nearby opponents back with force. Has a 0.5s cooldown.
- **Knockback**: All collisions push critters. Headbutts push harder.
- **Arena Collapse**: The outer ring of the arena disappears every 15 seconds, shrinking the playable area.
- **Elimination**: Fall off the arena edge and you're out.
- **Mass**: Heavier critters are harder to push but move slower.

## Win Conditions
- Last critter alive wins
- If time runs out, surviving critters share the win
